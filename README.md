# student-habits-academic-performance-analysis

##  Overview
This project explores how students' daily habits and lifestyle choices impact their academic performance. Using a data-driven approach, the analysis identifies key factors such as study habits, sleep patterns, diet quality, and social behaviors.

The project combines **Python-based exploratory data analysis (EDA)** with an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to deliver actionable insights for students, educators, and academic advisors.

---

##  Objective
- Analyze the relationship between student habits and academic performance  
- Identify key factors influencing exam scores  
- Provide data-driven insights to support better study and lifestyle decisions  
- Enable interactive exploration of student behavior patterns  

---

##  Motivation
Academic success is influenced by more than just study time. Factors such as:
- Sleep quality  
- Diet habits  
- Exercise frequency  
- Social life & part-time work  

play a critical role in performance.

This project aims to uncover how these variables interact and which ones have the greatest impact using data science techniques.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy) → Data cleaning & analysis  
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn** → Data visualization  
- **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard & reporting  
- **Jupyter Notebook** → EDA process  

---

## 📊 Dataset
- ~1,000 student records  
- Features include:
  - Study hours  
  - Sleep duration  
  - Diet quality  
  - Exercise habits  
  - Attendance  
  - Part-time job status  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 📈 Higher **attendance and study hours** strongly correlate with better exam scores  
- 😴 Students with **7+ hours of sleep** perform significantly better  
- 🥗 Better **diet quality** is associated with improved academic outcomes  
- 🏃 Regular **exercise** shows a positive impact on performance  
- 💼 Students with part-time jobs show slight variation in performance depending on workload
